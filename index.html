What is Money Explorers Camp?

Spark your child's curiosity about money in a fun and collaborative way! Our Money Explorers Camp introduces essential financial concepts through engaging activities and creative decision-making challenges. It's the perfect blend of learning and play.

Why Financial Literacy Matters Early

Studies show that children develop money habits as early as age 7. By introducing financial concepts in a fun, pressure-free environment, we help kids build a healthy relationship with money that lasts a lifetime.

What Your Child Will Learn

Our curriculum covers essential money concepts through fun, age-appropriate activities.

💰

The Story of Money

How we went from trading seashells to using credit cards, explored through a hands-on barter game.

🎯

Smart Spending & Value

Understanding what things cost, supply and demand, and making informed buying decisions.

📈

Saving & Investing

How banks work, compound interest, and picking stocks in real companies kids know and love.

🏪

Entrepreneurship & Creativity

Building their own store from scratch: designing products, setting prices, and selling to friends.

🛡️

Borrowing & Credit

What loans and credit cards really mean, and why paying back matters.

❤️

Giving & Taxes

The joy of generosity, how taxes work, and a neighborhood walk to see public services in action.
