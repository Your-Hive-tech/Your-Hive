Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Weekend Modules.

The Weekend Modules are designed for students ages 14–18 (grades 8–12). The curriculum tackles real adult financial situations in a way that's relevant and digestible for teens preparing for independence.
No — you can take individual modules that interest you. However, Module 1 (Money Systems 101) is a prerequisite for all other modules. Module 3 is also required before taking Modules 4 and 5.
Students who complete all 8 modules will sit a 25-question proctored exam. Those who pass receive a Certificate of Financial Literacy — a credential you can include on college applications or a résumé.
All sessions take place at the Learning Lab at 2846 NW 69th St, Seattle, WA.
Each module is $100. You pay per module, so you're only charged for what you sign up for.
Module 1 registration opens March 8, 2026 at 12pm PDT. Registration for Modules 2–7 opens March 9, 2026 at 12pm PDT.
