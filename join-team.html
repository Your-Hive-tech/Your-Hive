We are looking for motivated youth ages 16+ to join our team as interns. This role is designed for those ready to gain real-world experience and a head start on financial literacy.

What You'll Learn

You will gain practical skills that apply directly to your life, including:

📖

Financial Literacy

By supporting the facilitator, you will learn the basics of personal finance and gain knowledge that will benefit you for life.

⭐

Leadership Skills

Learn how to work with younger students and build a strong professional reputation that will serve you in future endeavors.

💬

Foundational Skills

Develop essential communication and teamwork abilities that form the foundation of success in any career or academic pursuit.

What You'll Get

In addition to valuable experience, you'll receive tangible benefits to support your future:

📄

Professional Letters of Recommendation

Stand out in your college applications and future job searches with a personalized letter of recommendation from our team.

  • Professional reference you can list on applications
  • Demonstrates real-world work experience to admissions committees
🏆

Certificate of Completion

Receive an official certificate documenting your internship experience and the skills you've developed.

  • Official recognition of your volunteer hours and commitment
  • Add to your resume, portfolio, or college applications

How to Apply

We value independence and initiative. Applicants must contact us directly to apply.

✉

Email us with a cover letter explaining why you are interested in this opportunity.

✉ internship@your-hive.com
